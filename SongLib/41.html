Text | 1 |

mama mana mandire raha niśi-din
kṛṣṇa murāri śrī kṛṣṇa murāri

Synonyms

mama:my ; mana:mind ; mandire:temple ; raha:stays ; niśi:night ; din:day ; kṛṣṇa:Krsna ; murāri:Killer of Mura ;

Translation

Please abide in the temple of my heart both day and night, O Krsna Murari, O Sri Krsna Murari!

Text | 2 |

bhakti pritī mālā candan
tumi nio he nio citta-nandan

Synonyms

bhakti:devotional service ; pritī:with love ; mālā:flower garland ; candan:sandalwood paste ; tumi:You ; nio:please accept ; he nio:O please accept ; citta ānandan:the happiness of the soul ;

Translation

Devotion, love, flower garlands, and sandalwood- please accept them, ODelighter of the Heart!

Text | 3 |

jīvana maraṇa tava pūja nivedan
sundara he mana-hāri

Synonyms

jīvana:in life ; maraṇa:in death ; tava:to You ; pūja:worship ; nivedan:offerings ; sundara:beautiful ; he mana-hāri:O stealer of the mind ;

Translation

3) In life or in death I worship You with these offerings,Beautiful One, O Enchanter of the Heart!

Text | 4 |

eso nanda-kumār ār nanda-kumār
habe prema-pradīpe āratī tomār

Synonyms

eso:come here ; nanda-kumār:son of Nanda Maharaja ; ār:and ; nanda-kumār:son of Nanda Maharaja ; habe:there will be ; prema:love ; pradīpe:light ; āratī:arati ceremony ; tomār:of Yours ;

Translation

Come, son of Nanda, and then, O Son of Nanda, I will offer Your arati ceremony with the lamplight of my love.

Text | 5 |

nayana jamunā jhare anibār
tomāra virahe giridhāri

Synonyms

nayana:eyes ; jamunā:the Yamuna River ; jhare:rains ; anibār:incessantly ; tomāra:of Yours ; virahe:separation ; giridhāri:Lifter of Govardhana Hill ;

Translation

5) The waters of the Yamuna River cascade incessantly from my eyes in your separation, O Holder of Govardhana Hill!

Text | 6 |

bandana gane tava bajūk jīvana
kṛṣṇa murāri śrī kṛṣṇa murāri

Synonyms

bandana:glorifications ; gane:counting ; tava:Yours ; bajūk:resounding ; jīvana:in life ;

Translation

6) May I pass my life absorbed only in songs of Your praise, O Krsna Murari, Sri Krsna Murari!
