Text | 1 |

(refrain)
sabse ūńcī prema sagāī

Synonyms

sabse:of all ; ūńcī:highest ; prema:love ;

Translation

Refrain: Of all forms of worship of the Lord, love is the highest.

Text | 2 |

sāga vidura ghara pāī

Synonyms

duryodhana:Duryodhana ; ko:of ; mevā:royal delicacies ; tyāgo:renounced ; sāga:vegetable preparations ; vidura:of Vidura ; ghara:home ; pāī:partook ;

Translation

Lord Krsna renounced the delicacies that Duryodhana offered, and ate vegetables at Vidura’s house.

Text | 3 |

Synonyms

As Lord Ramacandra, He ate fruits which were already filled with love from Sabari.

Text | 4 |

Synonyms

Translation

Love is indeed the best way to serve and please the Lord.

Text | 5 |

Synonyms

rājasuya yajña:the royal Rasasuya Yajna sacrifice ; yudhiṣṭhira:King Yudhisthira ; kīno:He did ; tāmai:Their (the guests present) ; jūṭha:partaken utensils ; uṭhāī:He lifted up ;

Text | 6 |

Text | 7 |
