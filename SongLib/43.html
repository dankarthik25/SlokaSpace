Text | 1 |

(Refrain)
murali manohara gopālā
govinda rādhe gopālā

Synonyms

murali:flute ; manohara:enchanting ; gopālā:maintainer of cows ; govinda:the One who gives pleasure to the cows senses ; rādhe:O Radha! ; gopālā:Gopala! ;

Translation

(Refrain) Glories to the One who enchants the world with His flute, Gopala! Govinda! Radhe Gopala!

Text | 2 |

paṇḍhari nāthā jaya hari viṭhṭhalā
jaya govardhana gopālā

Synonyms

paṇḍhari nāthā:Lord of Pandari ; jaya hari viṭhṭhalā:all glories to the Hari Vitthala (form of Lord Panduranga) ; jaya:all glories ; govardhana:Govardhana Hill ; gopālā:Gopala ;

Translation

Lord Pandari Nath! All Glories to Hari, who has a form of Sri Panduranga Vitthala! All Glories to the lifter of Govardhana Hill, Gopala!

Text | 3 |

jaya giridhāri kuñjabihāri
śyāma murāri gopālā

Synonyms

jaya:all glories ; giridhāri:lifter of Govardhana Hill ; kuñjabihāri:the one who wanders through the forest ; śyāma:dark complexioned ; murāri:killer of the demon Mura ;

Translation

2) All Glories to Giridhari, the One who roams through the forests of Vraja. All Glories to Syama, the killer of Mura, Gopala!

Text | 4 |

gopī vallabha dīna dayālā
devakī nandana gopālā

Synonyms

gopī vallabha:the lover of the cowherd girls ; dīna dayālā:the merciful one to the fallen souls ; devakī nandana:son of the Mother Devaki ; gopālā:Gopala ;

Translation

3) All Glories to the lover of gopis, the redeemer of the fallen. All Glories to the son of Mother Devaki, Gopala!
