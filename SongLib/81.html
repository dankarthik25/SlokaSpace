Text | 1 |

viditā-khila śāstra-sudhā jaladhē
mahit-opani ṣat kathit ārthanidhē |
hr̥dayē kalayē vimalaṁ caraṇaṁ
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 1||

Synonyms

viditā:known ; akhila:all ; śāstra:Shastras ; sudhā:amrutam/Nectar ; jaladha:Ocean ;
mahita:glorious ; upaniṣat:Upanishads (the end part of the Vedanta) ; kathita:conveyed ; artha:meaning ; nidhē:treasure ;
hr̥dayē:in the heart ; kalayē:meditate upon ; vimalaṁ:Stainless or Pure ; caraṇaṁ:feet ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

O the knower of all the nector-Ocean of Scriptures!
The expounder of the Knowledge of the great Upanisadic treasure-trove!
In my heart(I meditate) on Your pure lotus feet
Be my refuge O Master, Sankara!

Text | 2 |

karuṇā-varuṇā-laya pālaya māṁ
bhava-sāgara-duḥkha vidūna-hr̥dam |
racay ākhila darśana tattvavidaṁ
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 2||

Synonyms

karuṇā:Compassion ; varuṇā:Ocean ; ālaya:abode ; pālaya:Protect ; māṁ:me ;
bhavasāgara:Ocean of Samsara ; duḥkha:misery ; vidūna:distressed ; hr̥dam:heart ;
racaya:make ; akhila:all ; darśana:philosophy (about Parabhramam) ; tattva:the principles of the philosophy ; vidaṁ:knower ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

O (You who are) the ocean of compassion!
Save me whose heart is afflicted by the misery of the ocean of births
(By Your grace) make me the knower of the truths of all the systems of philosophy.
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.

Text | 3 |

bhavatā janatā suhitā bhavitā
nija-bodha vicāraṇa cārumatē |
kalayēśvara jīva vivēka-vidaṁ
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 3||

Synonyms

bhavatā:by You ; janatā:the people ; suhitā:agreeable/suitable (for practicing Dharma) ; bhavitā:become ;
nija:one’s own/self ; bōdha:knowledge ; vicāraṇa:to enquire ; cārumatē:excellent / beautiful mindset ;
kalaya:make/perform ; ēśvara:Parabhraman ; jīva:Jiva ; vivēka:discrimination ; vidaṁ:knower ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

The people have found happiness due to You,
who have the intellect adept in the inquiry into Self-knowledge.
Make me understand the knowledge of God and the soul.
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.

Text | 4 |

bhava eva bhavāniti mē nitarāṁ
samajāyata cētasi kautukitā |
mama vāraya moha mahā-jaladhiṁ
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 4||

Synonyms

bhava:Parameshwara ; ēva:only ; bhavān:yourself ; iti:thus ; mē:to me ;
nitarāṁ:very much ; samajāyata:arose ; cētasi:mind ; kautukitā:curiosity/eagerness ;
mama:my ; vāraya:remove/destroy ; mōha:delusion ; mahājaladhiṁ:great ocean ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

Knowing that you (Adi Shankar) is Lord Shiva,
Knowing thismy heart is overwhelming(filled) with bliss of joy.
Protect me from the vast ocean of delusion.
Be Thou my refuge, O Master, Sankara.

Text | 5 |

sukr̥tē’ dhikr̥tē bahudhā bhavato
bhavitā sama-darśana lālasatā |
atidīnam-imaṁ paripālaya mām
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 5||

Synonyms

sukr̥tē:good Karma/Punya ; adhikr̥tē:increases ; bahudhā:in many ways/ respects ; bhavatah:from you ;
bhavitā:happen ; sama-darśana:equanimity of mind ; lālasatā:being very eager/thirsty ;
ati:very ; dīnam:wretched (in terms of stock of Punya) ; imaṁ:this fellow/me ; pari-pālaya:protect in every way ; māṁ:me ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

Only after numerous virtuous deeds have been performed in many ways
does a keen desire for the experience of Brahman through You arise.
Protect (me who am) extremely helpless.
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.

Text | 6 |

jagatī mavituṁ kalitā kr̥tayo
vicaranti mahā mah-asaś chalataḥ |
ahi-māṁ śurivātra vibhāsi guro
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 6||

Synonyms

jagatī:this world ; avituṁ:for protecting ; kalita:adorned ; ākr̥tayō:various shapes and forms ;
vicaranti:move around ; mahā:great ; mahasa:shine/brilliance ; chalataḥ:pretend/cheating (disguising their appearance) ;
ahi-māṁ-śuhu:sun ; iva:like ; atra:right infront of me ; vibhāsi:shining ; gurō:oh Guru ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

For the sake of saving the world,
(Your) great (disciples) wander assuming various forms and guises.
O Guru, You shine like the sun (among them).
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.

Text | 7 |

guru-puṁgava puṁgava-kētana tē
samatā mayatāṁ nahi ko’pi sudhīḥ |
śaraṇāgata vatsala tattvanidhē
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 7||

Synonyms

guru:Guru ; puṅgava:bull ; kētana:flag ; tē:of you ;
samatā:equality ; mayatāṁ:being ; nahi:no ; ko’pi:who else ; sudhīḥ:brilliant intellect ;
śaraṇe:Protection / refuge ; āgatah:arrival/come under ; vatsala:kind (like parents towards child) ; tattva:truth ; nidhē:treasure ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

O Best among the Gurus! The Lord whose flag bears the emblem of the bull!
You have no equal among the wise.
You who are affectionate to those who seek refuge! The treasure of truth!
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.

Text | 8 |

viditā na mayā viśadaika kalā
na ca kiṁcana kāñcanamasti guro
drutamēva vidhēhi kr̥pāṁ sahajāṁ
bhava śaṁkara dēśika mē śaraṇam || 8||

Synonyms

viditā:known ; na:not ; mayā:by me ; viśada:clearly ; eka:one ; kalā:art ;
na:not ; ca:and ; kiñcana:even a little bit ; kāñcana:Gold ; asti:is ; gurō:oh Guro! ;
drutamēva:quickly ; vidhēhi:show ; kr̥pāṁ:compassion ; sahajāṁ:inborn nature ;
bhava:You become ; śaṅkara:Oh Shankara! ; mē:for me ; śaraṇam:refuge ;

Translation

I have neither understood even one branch of knowledge clearly
nor do I possess any wealth, O Guru.
Quickly bestow on me the compassion which is natural to You.
O Preceptor Shankara, be my refuge.
