Text | 1 |

(refrain)
jaya rādhe jaya kṛṣṇa jaya vṛndāvana
rasika mukuṭa maṇī jaya gopī-gaṇa

Synonyms

jaya:all glories ; rādhe:Srimati Radharani ; jaya:all glories ; kṛṣṇa:Sri Krsna ; jaya:all glories ; vṛndāvana:Vrndavana ; rasika:the esoteric mellows ; mukuṭa:crown ; maṇī:jewel ; jaya:all glories ; gopī-gana:the assembly of cowherd girls ;

Translation

(Refrain): All glories to Sri Radha! All glories to Sri Krsna! All glories to their abode of Vrndavana! All glories to the gopis who are the jewel of the crown of the rasika mood.

Text | 2 |

rādhe rādhe rādhe śyāma rādhe rādhe śyāma
śyāma rādhe
śyāma yugala nāma mero hai jīvana

Synonyms

rādhe rādhe rādhe śyāma rādhe rādhe śyāma śyāma:Radhe Radhe Radhe Syama, Radhe Radhe Syama Syama ; rādhe śyāma:Radhe Syama ; yugala:the divine couple ; nāma:name ; mero:my ; hai:is ; jīvana:life ;

Translation

In this ecstatic mood, Krsna sings “Radhe Radhe” and Radha sings “Syam Syam!” The Holy Name and the divine pair of Radhe Syam are my life and soul.

Text | 3 |

mero eka prāṇa-dhana eka hī hai jīvana
nanda-nandana madana mohana rādhikā-ramaṇa

Synonyms

mero:my ; eka:one ; prāna:life ; dhana:wealth ; eka:one ; hī:certainly ; hai:is ; jīvana:life ; nanda-nandana:the son of Nanda Maharaja ; madana mohana:the one who enchants Cupid ; rādhika-ramaṇa:the one who gives pleasure to Srimati Radharani ;

Translation

I only have one treasure within this life. The son of Nanda Maharaja, the enchanter of Cupid, and pleasure giver to Radharani are my life and soul.

Text | 4 |

śyāma hī so mero pyāro śyāma hī mero bharatāra
śyāma hī samāyo atho yama tana mana

Synonyms

śyāma:Syama (Krsna) ; hī:certainly ; so:like ; mero:mine ; pyāro:beloved ; śyāma:Syama ; hī:certainly ; mero:my ; bharatara:husband ; śyāma:Syama ; hī:certainly ; samāyo:immersed ; atho:likewise ; yāma:in the path ; tana mana:in mind and body ;

Translation

Syama is the only person who I love, He is certainly my husband. My mind and body is perpetually immersed in Syama!

Text | 5 |

maiń to hūń vahiń sācārī rādherāṇi
kī nitya dhāma vṛndāvana mahārāṇī kī

Synonyms

maiń:I ; to:certainly ; hūń:am ; vahiń:that ; sācārī:servant ; rādherāni:Radharani ; kī:of ; nitya:eternal ; dhāma:abode ; vṛndāvana:Vrndavana ; mahārānī:great queen ; kī:of ;

Translation

I am verily the maidservant of Srimati Radharani, who is the eternal queen of the eternal abode of Vrndavana.

Text | 6 |

nitya dhāma nitya sevā nitya pāūń atho yama
rākho rūcī soī joī ṭhakurānī kī

Synonyms

nitya:eternal ; dhāma:abode ; nitya:eternal ; sevā:service ; nitya:eternal ; pāūń:I obtain ; atho:likewise ; yāma:in the path ; rākho:I maintain ; rūcī:Her will ; ṭhakurānī:as the Lady Lord ; kī:of ;

Translation

My wish is to obtain a daily practice of doing eternal service in the eternal abode. Thus, I wish to keep my life in the way my master, Srimati Radharani, desires.
