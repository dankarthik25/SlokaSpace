Text | 1 |

(Refrain)
hari tum haro jana kī pīr

Synonyms

hari:Lord Hari ; tum:You ; haro:take ; jana:people ; kī:of ; pīr:suffering ;

Translation

Refrain: Oh Hari! Please remove the sufferings of humanity.

Text | 2 |

draupadī kī lāj rākhī
tum baḍhāyo cīr

Synonyms

draupadī:Queen Draupadi ; kī:of ; lāj:modesty ; rākhī:kept ; tum:You ; baḍhāyo:expanded ; cīr:clothing ;

Translation

1) You protected the honor of Draupadi by increasing the length of her cloth.

Text | 3 |

bhakta kārana rūpa narahari
dharyo āp śarīr

Synonyms

bhakta:devotee ; kārana:for the sake of ; rūpa:form ; narahari:Lord Nrsimhadeva ; dharyo:have donned ; āp:You ; śarīr:body ;

Translation

2-For the sake of Your devotee, You assumed the form of Nrsimhadeva. You killed Hiranyakasipu with Your fierce form.

Text | 5 |

Synonyms

būḍate:drowning ; gaja:elephant ; rāja:king of ; rākhyo:kept ; kiyo:You have done ; bāhar:out of ; nīr:river ;

Text | 6 |

dāsī mīrā lāl giradhar
duḥkha jahāń tahāń pīr

Synonyms

dāsī:maidservant ; mīrā:Mira Bai ; lāl:darling ; giradhar:Giridhar, lifter of Govardhana Hill ; duḥkha:pain ; jahāń:where ; tahāń:there ;

Translation

5) Oh darling Giridhara! I am only Your maidservant, Mira. Here and there, there is only suffering and pain. Therefore, Oh Hari! Please remove the sufferings of humanity.
