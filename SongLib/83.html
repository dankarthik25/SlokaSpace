nā mōhamu tīradā aruṇācalā | 86 |

Translation

86.Oh Arunachala, you removed my attachment to me,
Put in there attachment to you, and by your grace,
Keep that attachment from not vanishing.

87.Oh Arunachala , Is it divine silence,
When one is silent and immobile like a stone?

89.Oh Arunachala, who was it that destroyed

90.Oh Arunachala, I told it this way because,

91.Oh Arunachala , let us both enjoy this divine happiness,

93.Oh Arunachala, what is the profit got buy you,

95.Oh Arunachala, The moment I came in, when you called,

96.Oh Arunachala, To leave you is difficult,

97.Oh Arunachala, you drew me out of my home,

98.Oh Arunachala, I have published your acts now,

101.Oh Arunachala, like the hail stones melting in rain,

102.Oh Arunachala, as soon as I thought of Arunachala,
