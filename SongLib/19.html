Text | 1 |

gaurāńga karuṇā koro dīna hīna jane
mosamo patita prabhu nāhi tribhūvane

Synonyms

gaurāńga:Lord Gauranga ; karuṇā:mercy ; koro:please do ; dīna:fallen ; hīna:deprived ; jane:persons ; mo samo:just like me ; patita:fallen ; prabhu:O Lord ; nāhi:there is no ; tribhūvane:in the three worlds ;

Translation

O my dear Lord Gauranga! Please show Your mercy to this lowly and destitute soul. O Lord! There is no one more fallen than myself in all the three worlds.

Text | 2 |

dante tṛṇa dhari gaura ḍāki he tomāre
kṛpā kari eso āmār hṛdaya mandire

Synonyms

dante:teeth ; tṛṇa:grass ; dhari(yā):beholding ; gaura:Lord Caitanya ; ḍāki:I am calling ; he:O ; tomāre:to You ; kṛpā:mercy ; kari(yā):doing ; eso:please come ; āmār:my ; hṛdaya:heart ; mandire:in the temple ;

Translation

Holding grass between my teeth, O Lord Gaura, I am calling out to You now! Please be compassionate upon me and come to reside within the temple of my heart.

Text | 3 |

jadi dayā nā karibe patita dekhiyā
patita pāvana nāma kisera lāgiyā

Synonyms

yadi:if ; dayā:mercy ; nā:no ; karibe:You will do ; patita:fallen ; dekhiyā:seeing ; patita pāvana:the redeemer of the fallen ; nāma:name ; kisera:why ;

Translation

If You do not give Your mercy, seeing how fallen I am, then why are You known as Patita Pavana, the merciful Savior of the fallen?

Text | 4 |

paḍechi bhava tuphāne nāhika nistāra
śrī caraṇa taraṇī dāne dāse kara pāra

Synonyms

paḍechi:I have fallen into ; bhava:misery ; tuphāne:hurricane ; nāhika:I do not have ; nistāra:relief ; śrī caraṇa:His divine lotus feet ; taraṇī:boat ; dāne:gift ; dāse:to this servant ; kara:do ; pāra:redeem (save) ;

Translation

I am plunged amidst the violent hurricane-stricken waves in the ocean of this material world, from which there is no escape. Kindly give me the gift of Your divine lotus feet, which are compared to a boat in which Your servant may cross over the ocean of birth and death.

Text | 5 |

śrī kṛṣṇa caitanya prabhu dāser anudāsa
prārthanā karaye sadā narottama dāsa

Synonyms

śrī kṛṣṇa caitanya:Sri Krsna Caitanya ; prabhu:Lord ; dāser:of the servant ; anudāsa:servant [of the servant] ; prārthanā:prayers ; karaye:offering ; sadā:always ; narottama dāsa:Narottama Dasa Thakura ;

Translation

Narottama Dasa, the servant of the servant of Lord Sri Krsna Caitanya Prabhu, ceaselessly makes this prayer.
